Our Story

Born from late-night food adventures and early morning market visits, Street Eats Malaysia emerged from our collective obsession with the incredible diversity of flavors found on every Malaysian street corner.

What started as weekend food hunts among friends has evolved into a passionate mission to document, celebrate, and preserve the culinary traditions that make Malaysia a true food paradise.

Every recommendation comes from personal experience, every story honors the vendors who create magic daily, and every recipe respects the heritage it represents


-- Street Eats Editor

Authentic flavors from generations of tradition

Our Mission


To preserve and promote Malaysia’s street food heritage.


We believe every bowl of laksa, every skewer of satay, and every packet of nasi lemak tells a story worth sharing.

Our Vision


To become the go-to hub for discovering, learning, and enjoying Malaysian street food — both online and in person.


Discover, learn, and enjoy Malaysian street food — both online and in person. Whether you’re a curious traveler or a homesick Malaysian abroad, we’re here to bring the streets to you.
